About This Analysis

10,000 scenarios are simulated using your actual win rate, average win, and average loss from closed CSP & Bull Put Spread trades.

Each trade samples a P&L from a normal distribution centred on your historical average, then applies it to 2% of the current account balance.

Results show the range of realistic outcomes over the specified trading horizon.
ERROR-tagged trades are excluded. CSV refreshes automatically each page load.
